Khagrachari

Destination Details

Khagrachari (Bengali: খাগড়াছড়ি) often called the "Land of Heavenly Beauty", is a serene district in the Chittagong Hill Tracts of Bangladesh. It is known for its verdant hills, waterfalls, and vibrant indigenous cultures. Key attractions include Alutila Cave, Richhang Waterfall, Hanging Bridge, and Sajek Valley, which is famed for its panoramic views of mist-covered mountains. The area is a haven for nature enthusiasts and offers a peaceful escape into the unspoiled beauty of the hill tracts.
