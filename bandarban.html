Bandarban

Destination Details

Bandarban (Bengali: বান্দরবান), located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. The region boasts lush green hills, cascading waterfalls, dense forests, and vibrant tribal culture. Key attractions include Nilgiri Hills, Sangu River, Boga Lake, Ruma Waterfall, and the picturesque Trekking Trails. The serene landscapes, mist-covered mountains, and unique wildlife make Bandarbans a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. Its tranquil beauty and cultural richness offer a peaceful retreat for those seeking a getaway.
