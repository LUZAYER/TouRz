Saint Martins Island

Destination Details

Saint Martins Island (সেন্টমার্টিন দ্বীপ) is a small coral island in the northeastern part of the Bay of Bengal, about 9 km south of the tip of the Cox's Bazar-Teknaf peninsula, and forming the southernmost part of Bangladesh. It has an area of only 3 km2. There is a small adjoining island that is separated at high tide, called Chhera Dwip. It is about 8 kilometres (5 miles) west of the northwest coast of Myanmar, at the mouth of the Naf River. It's the only coral island of Bangladesh.
